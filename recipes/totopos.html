Tangy Totopos

Description

I learned this recipe from my amazing brother. It consists of your good ol' totopos and a bootleg version of salsa negra. This sauce is commonly mixed with beer or used to season steaks!

This version of the sauce tends to be extremly tangy because of the huge amounts of lime juice that I use, but as a lime lover myself, I love this recipe!

Ingredients

  • A bowl filled with Totopos
  • 1 lime
  • Valentina sauce
  • Maggi sauce
  • Tajín Seasoning

Steps

  1. First, pour the juice of the lime on the totopos.
  2. Next, add the Valentina sauce and Maggi sauce to the bowl.
  3. Finally, add a lot of Tajín seasoning aaaand enjoy!
